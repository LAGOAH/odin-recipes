Salad

Description

Salad is a fresh and healthy dish that typically consists of a mixture of raw or cooked vegetables, fruits, nuts, seeds, and sometimes protein like chicken, fish, or tofu. It's often dressed with a flavorful vinaigrette or creamy dressing, making it a versatile and delicious option for a light meal or side dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup olives (optional)
  • 1/4 cup toasted nuts (walnuts, almonds)
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinaigrette dressing

    • Steps

    1. In a large salad bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, and red onion.
    2. Add crumbled feta cheese and olives (if using) on top of the salad.
    3. Sprinkle toasted nuts over the salad for added crunch.
    4. Drizzle balsamic vinaigrette dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine all ingredients.
    5. Serve immediately as a light meal or side dish.
